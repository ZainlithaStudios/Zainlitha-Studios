Our Games

Anele

">
Arena Brawler 2.5D Fighting PC

Story: Anele is a young woman fighting in illegal pit arenas of a dystopian slum to rescue her kidnapped parents. Her fight exposes the broken systems and silent brutality of life in "The Stack."

Style: Afro-grit aesthetic with 1v1 arena brawling, improvised weapons, and finishers tied to African music genres like Amapiano, gqom, and industrial house.

Impact: Rooted in South African life - taxi slang, slum survival, underground culture. A bold narrative about class, survival, and the power of resistance. #JointheMove

Zizi's Quest: Spirits of the Land

2D/3D Adventure PC & Console Flagship Title

Setting: A post-apocalyptic South Africa that blends cultural heritage and supernatural elements.

Story: Follow Zizi, a young woman guided by ancestral voices, as she journeys through iconic South African landscapes from the Drakensberg mountains to abandoned Johannesburg. She must reclaim lost knowledge, reconnect with her roots, and restore balance to a fractured land.

What Makes It Special: Authentic South African traditions woven into gameplay, story-first design that prioritizes narrative over mechanics, and emotional reflection on cultural loss, resilience, and identity. This game serves as both entertainment and cultural preservation.

About ZAINLITHA Studios

Our Mission

To reimagine and preserve African cultural heritage through innovative storytelling and digital gaming, while empowering young creators in Africa.

Our Vision

To become the leading African studio in culturally rooted, globally recognized video games that inspire learning, identity, and pride.

Company Info

Legal Name: Zainlitha Studios (Pty) Ltd
Type: Creative Tech & Game Development Studio
Founded: 2025
Location: Cape Town, South Africa (Remote Operations)
Certification: B-BBEE Level 1 Certificate ✅

Our Approach

We sit at the intersection of entertainment, education, and digital heritage preservation. Our games blend African narratives with modern gaming technology to create experiences that are both commercially viable and culturally significant.
